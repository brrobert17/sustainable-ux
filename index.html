The Ocean's Voice

I cover 70% of your planet. I regulate your climate, provide food for billions, and generate over half the oxygen you breathe. Yet my health is declining rapidly due to pollution, overfishing, and climate change.

"I hold life within me, but I am drowning in plastic."

My Values

  • Marine biodiversity - protecting the thousands of species that call me home
  • Pollution reduction - eliminating the 8 million tons of plastic that enter me annually
  • Clean water - maintaining the quality needed for all life to thrive

My Challenges

Plastic Pollution

Every year, 8 million tons of plastic waste enters my waters, harming marine life and ecosystems.

Overfishing

Unsustainable fishing practices are depleting my fish populations faster than they can reproduce.

Climate Change

Rising temperatures are causing acidification, coral bleaching, and disrupting marine ecosystems.

How You Can Help

Reduce Plastic

Choose reusable alternatives to single-use plastics and properly recycle what you can't avoid.

Choose Sustainable Seafood

Look for certifications like MSC or ASC when purchasing seafood to support responsible fishing.

Support Ocean-Safe Products

Buy from brands that use ocean-safe materials and reduce microplastic shedding.

Choose Eco-Tourism

When traveling, select operators that protect marine ecosystems rather than exploit them.

Join Our Movement

Sign up to receive updates on our campaigns and learn how you can make a difference for the oceans.
